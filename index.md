## Abstract

In general, people's beliefs and perceptions of reality can be considered an imporant part of human beings in their considertion and choices. People's beliefs and cnvictions can be a result of the influnces of people in society and the experieces of the individual throughot his or her life. People often prefer the opinions and beliefs of others to their own and in a way try to coply with the norms, values, and beliefs generally accepted by socety in their daily deisions. This is not only true for indivduals, but also for organizations. In the real world, organizations seek to match the featres of their products with the needs and desires of individuals in soiety as much as possible. In other words, the opiions and beliefs of companies' end consumers have an important impact on the type and atitude of companies in producing and presnting their products to their customers. With the growth and devlopment of social networks and the faster exchange of information, people exchange information and opinins on various topics not only in the traditonal market space but also in the new space of the Internet. Therefore, these changes and developments have caused a change in the attiude of companies in rceiving the needs of their customers. This project aims to be part of the solotion for companies to have better understanding towards the customers needs in order to improve their contribtions for the customers and socity. This project is focused on the smarthone rewees and the amazon website is choosen to be source of the reviews collection. The project begins with the application of scrapper in order to capture the reviews using xx and xx libreries (This part needs to figure it out!). In the next step the collected data is visualized using pandas library. As follow, the pre pre-processing have started in order to do further analysis and reduce the noises using re library. The normalized textual data is analysed using nltk library to determine the sentiment of each user regarding the product.
